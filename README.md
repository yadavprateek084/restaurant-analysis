# 🍽️ Restaurant Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is part of my **Data Analysis Internship** at Cognifyz Technologies.
The objective was to analyze restaurant datasets and uncover meaningful insights related to cuisines, customer ratings, price ranges, online delivery, restaurant chains, and customer reviews.

The project focuses on applying **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**, **Data Visualization**, and **Business Intelligence** techniques to solve real-world business problems.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The main objectives of this project were:

* Analyze restaurant distribution across different cities
* Identify the most common cuisines and cuisine combinations
* Understand pricing patterns among restaurants
* Evaluate the effect of online delivery and table booking on ratings
* Analyze customer voting behavior
* Extract insights from customer reviews
* Perform location-based restaurant analysis

## 📊 Tasks Performed

### **Level 1 Analysis**

### 1️⃣ Top Cuisines Analysis

* Identified the top 3 most common cuisines
* Calculated their percentage share

### 2️⃣ City Analysis

* Found the city with the highest number of restaurants
* Calculated average restaurant ratings by city
* Identified top-rated cities

### 3️⃣ Price Range Distribution

* Visualized price range distribution using bar charts
* Calculated percentage of restaurants in each category

### 4️⃣ Online Delivery Analysis

* Calculated percentage of restaurants offering online delivery
* Compared average ratings between delivery and non-delivery restaurants

---

## 📈 Level 2 Analysis

### 5️⃣ Restaurant Ratings Analysis

* Studied aggregate rating distribution
* Identified the most common rating ranges
* Calculated average customer votes

### 6️⃣ Cuisine Combination Analysis

* Found the most common cuisine pairings
* Analyzed their rating trends

### 7️⃣ Geographic Analysis

* Mapped restaurant locations using coordinates
* Identified location clusters

### 8️⃣ Restaurant Chain Analysis

* Identified restaurant chains
* Compared popularity and ratings across chains

---

## 🔍 Level 3 Analysis

### 9️⃣ Restaurant Reviews Analysis

* Extracted common positive and negative keywords
* Analyzed review length patterns

### 🔟 Votes Analysis

* Identified restaurants with highest and lowest votes
* Studied correlation between votes and ratings

### 1️⃣1️⃣ Price Range vs Services Analysis

* Analyzed the relationship between price range, online delivery, and table booking
* Explored whether premium restaurants provide better services

---

## 📌 Key Insights

* Certain cuisines dominate the restaurant market.
* Cities with higher restaurant density do not always have better ratings.
* Mid-range restaurants are the most common.
* Restaurants with online delivery often receive more customer engagement.
* Strong correlation exists between votes and ratings.
* Popular restaurant chains tend to maintain consistent ratings.
